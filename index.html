More About Me

My name is Monique Geldenhuys and I am currently enrolled for a Full Stack Web Development course. I am a 24 year old who would love to start a career in tech even though I have little to none experience in coding. I am precise with my work and I love to learn new things. For me it is important to do what you love, and mine is to have challenges.

In my free time, I love to read or spend time outdoors. I am a naturelover and any time spent outside is a definite mood booster! Spending time outdoors can be anything from just chilling outside to horse riding, to hiking, to cycling, or anything active!

I am an introvert by nature, but can get talkative the moment I get comfortable with the people around me. Or if I have to do a pitch and I know exactly what I'm talking about, there is no introvert in sight!


Why Choose A Career In Tech?

With tech, you are constantly confronted with errors or if you have one syntax wrong, then nothing will work. So to get everything perfect can be quite challenging, and that is enough brain stimulation for me! During the course at HyperionDev, I have learned that you need to get used to feeling "stuck". When you can't figure something out, just walk around or keep your mind occupied with something else. The solution normally comes when you least expect it!

For me the part I enjoy most of coding, is to see the outcome and knowing I did it! This career can be quite rewarding if you have the patience to work through the "stuck" phase.
